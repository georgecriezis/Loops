1. Retail Checkout Simulation

- Acts as a cash register, prompting you to enter prices for items.

-Uses a list to store prices and keeps asking for input until you enter 0.

-Calculates the total bill, the average price per item, and the total count of items.

2. Market Survey Analyzer

-Takes a list of survey responses representing customer preferences.

-Uses a dictionary to count how many votes each item received.

-Calculates the percentage of total votes for each item to show market share.

3. Expense Report Categorizer

-Organizes spending by grouping costs into categories like "Travel" or "Meals".

-Uses a nested dictionary to hold lists of expenses under each category header.

-Loops through the data to calculate subtotals per category and a final grand total.

4. Sales Commission Calculator

-Uses a custom function to calculate a 10% commission based on sales data.

-Stores results in a dictionary and sorts them to create a leaderboard.

-Prints the leaderboard in ranked order from highest earner to lowest.

5. Stock Portfolio Tracker

-Manages investments using a nested dictionary to store shares and prices per stock.

-Calculates the value of each stock by multiplying shares owned by the current price.

-Sums all individual stock values to show the total worth of the portfolio.

6. Bank Loan Repayment Simulator

-Takes input for loan amount, interest rate, and monthly payment.

-Uses a while loop to simulate month-by-month debt reduction until the balance is zero.

-Calculates interest monthly before subtracting the payment from the balance.

7. Simple Supply Chain Tracker

-Models a company with inventory spread across multiple warehouse locations.

-Uses nested loops to visit each warehouse and scan its inventory dictionary.

-Totals up the amount of each specific product available company-wide.

8. Customer Loyalty Tiers

-Evaluates customer spending data stored in a dictionary.

-Assigns tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold) based on set spending thresholds.

-Produces a summary report showing the total count of customers in each tier.

9. Business Growth Projection

-Takes initial revenue and a growth rate percentage as input.

-Uses a for loop to compound the revenue over a set number of years.

-Displays the year-by-year projected growth in a structured table.

10. Startup Pitch Deck Visualizer

-Converts numerical revenue data into a visual bar chart.

-Uses string multiplication to create bars (#) proportional to the revenue values.

-Provides a quick visual representation of growth trends directly in the terminal.
